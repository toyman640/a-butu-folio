Project detail

In response to immediate challenges faced by families across the state, we undertook the distribution of relief care packages. These packages contained essential items that aimed to alleviate some of the hardships faced by those in need. By reaching out to various corners of the state with these packages, we not only provided practical assistance but also fostered a sense of unity and support during difficult times.
